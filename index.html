DLmath Lab

Korea University, Department of Mathematics

DLmath Lab is the Deep Learning and Mathematics research lab at Korea University.

The Deep Learning and Mathematics (DLmath) Lab is composed of graduate students
from the Department of Mathematics at Korea University.

Our goal is to gain a mathematical understanding
of the theoretical foundations of deep learning.

We also provide deep learning consulting to industry partners
looking to adopt AI-driven solutions and modernize their systems.
